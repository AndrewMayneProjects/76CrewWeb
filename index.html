Our Mission

Preserving the voice of the people.

In an age where discourse is increasingly curated and controlled, 76 Crew stands as a bulwark for the free exchange of ideas. The marketplace of ideas—messy, contentious, and free—remains the surest path to truth.

Principles

The bedrock values that guide us.

Freedom requires responsibility. We are guided by foundational ideals drawn from the spirit of 1776.

I. Free Expression

The First Amendment is the foundation of a free society. We defend the right of every individual to speak their mind.

II. Open Discourse

Truth emerges from debate, not decree. We champion the free exchange of ideas—even those we disagree with.

III. Individual Liberty

Government exists to secure rights, not grant them. We stand for the inherent dignity and freedom of every person.

IV. Civic Virtue

Freedom requires responsibility. We cultivate informed, engaged citizens who understand their rights and duties.
